System Overview

Darkenyas CRM is built around organisations (Organisors), agents, and leads. Access to each module depends on your role (Administrator, Organisor, or Agent).

Lead Management

Leads with agent assignment, source & value categories, and activity history. Filter by organisation and agent (Admin/Organisor).

Team Management

Organisors (organisations) and Agents. Role-based access: Admin manages all; Organisor manages own organisation and agents.

Products & Stock

Product catalog (category/subcategory), stock levels, bulk price update. Sales dashboard and product charts (Organisor/Admin).

Order Management

Orders linked to leads and products. Create, update, cancel, delete. Active/completed/cancelled views; filters by organisation and agent.

Finance

Financial reports by date range: earnings, order totals, cost and profit. Filter by organisation and agent (Admin/Organisor); Agent sees own orders only.

Tasks & Notifications

Tasks with assignee, due date and priority. Notifications for new orders, task assignments, lead assignments, and scheduled reminders.

Activity Log

Audit trail of actions (leads, orders, tasks, organisors, agents). Admin sees all; Organisor sees organisation; Agent sees own and affecting them.

Authentication & Security

Signup, email verification, login, password reset and logout. Role-based permissions throughout the system.
